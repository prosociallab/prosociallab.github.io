Paper 2 Card tool

Translating academic insights into design cards
We developed a tool that allows you to translate any academic papers into a card-based resource that can help with your design problems. This tool attemps to help make scholarly research more accessible, and is inspired by the growing set of card-based design tools, or design cards. Our paper was published at the ACM Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems.
