Prosocial Lab, a research group at the University of Washington, studies, designs, and builds technologies that foster and support positive and prosocial behaviors – to benefit individuals and society as a whole.

Through our work, we seek to contribute to our fundamental understanding of people, as well as develop design guidelines and tools to facilitate technology design.
